Methodology (v2)

  • Zone classification: Postcode-based using actual rate card suburb/postcode→zone mapping. Suburb IDs from tucJobArchive.ucjbTo mapped to PostCode via tucSuburb table, then PostCode→Zone from rate card spreadsheet.
  • Base rate calculation: 2017-2020: ucjbAmount − fuelsurchargeAmount (rawBaseAmount was null). 2021: rawBaseAmount where available, else subtraction. 2022+: rawBaseAmount directly.
  • Parent/child filtering: Pre-2024: all standalone jobs (rel=1). 2024-2025: delivery children only (rel=20, speed 94/95) — parent jobs excluded as they bundle pickup+linehaul.
  • Thresholds: Base rate $4–$200 to exclude pickup legs and data errors.
  • Scope: Auckland (client 16867), Sundays only, Speed 95 (Home Delivery) + Speed 4 (3-Hour, pre-2019).
  • 2025: Partial year (Jan–Mar).
  • v1 preserved: zone-prices.html (price-ratio classification)
